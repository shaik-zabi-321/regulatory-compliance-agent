EMPLOYEE & CUSTOMER DATA POLICY (Last updated 2015)

Section 1: Data Collection
The company collects customer names, addresses, phone numbers, and purchase history for marketing and internal analytics. Consent is assumed upon use of our website or store loyalty card; no separate consent form is required.

Section 2: Marketing Communications
Customers will receive promotional emails and SMS messages unless they call our support line to request removal. There is no online unsubscribe link at this time.

Section 3: Data Storage
All customer data is stored indefinitely on company servers. There is no scheduled deletion process.

Section 4: Third-Party Sharing
Customer data may be sold to third-party marketing companies to generate additional revenue. This is disclosed only in the printed terms and conditions available at physical store locations.

Section 5: Data Breach Response
In case of a data breach, the company will notify affected customers "as soon as reasonably possible," with no fixed timeline specified.
