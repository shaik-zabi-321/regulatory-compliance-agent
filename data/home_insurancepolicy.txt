HOME INSURANCE POLICY (Sample Policy Document)

Section 1: Coverage for Water Damage
This policy covers water damage caused by burst pipes or appliance leaks inside the home. Flood damage from external sources, such as rising rivers, heavy rainfall, or storm surge, is not covered under this policy.

Section 2: Fire and Smoke Damage
This policy covers damage to the home and personal belongings caused by fire, including smoke damage. Damage caused by wildfires occurring within 1 mile of the property is covered up to $50,000.

Section 3: Claim Filing Timeline
Policyholders must file a claim within 90 days of the incident. Claims filed after this period may be denied.

Section 4: Premium Increases
The insurer may increase premiums annually by up to 15% without prior written notice to the policyholder, other than the renewal statement sent 30 days before the policy renews.

Section 5: Cancellation Policy
The insurer may cancel a policy at any time for non-payment, with a 10-day notice period. The policyholder may not cancel mid-term without a cancellation fee equal to 20% of the remaining premium.
