COOKIE POLICY (Sample Website)

1. What Are Cookies
Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

2. Cookies We Use
We use the following types of cookies:
- Essential cookies (required for the website to function)
- Analytics cookies (to understand how visitors use our site)
- Advertising cookies (to show you relevant ads)

3. How We Get Your Consent
By continuing to browse our website, you agree to our use of cookies. A banner will appear on your first visit informing you that the site uses cookies.

4. Managing Cookies
You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. We do not currently provide an in-site option to accept or reject specific categories of cookies.

5. Third-Party Cookies
We allow select advertising partners to place cookies on your device to deliver personalized ads. These partners may track your activity across other websites.
